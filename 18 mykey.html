Choosing your Public Key

Your public key is a 5 (or more) word phrase that will identify you to Calipso. Your Ai is designed to give information to you and only you - so this key phrase is important. While there are more verification steps, you can consider this phrase as an activation code. You want to keep this activation code private in case your access needs to be recovered.
